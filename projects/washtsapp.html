WASHtsApp Privacy Policy

Introduction

WASHtsApp is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our app.

Information Collection

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, such as messages sent, received, and deleted.

We collect this information through your use of the app.

Information Use

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the WASHtsApp service.
  • To communicate with you about the app, including updates, promotions, and customer support.
  • To analyze and improve the app's performance and user experience.
  • To protect the security and integrity of the app and its users.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Third-party service providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the app, such as cloud storage providers, analytics providers, and customer support providers.
  • Law enforcement authorities: We may share your information with law enforcement authorities if required by law or to protect the safety of our users or the public.

We require these third parties to maintain appropriate security measures and to only use your information for the purposes for which it was provided.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption: We use encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest.
  • Access controls: We limit access to your information to authorized personnel.
  • Regular monitoring: We regularly monitor our systems for security vulnerabilities and take appropriate steps to address them.

User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to your personal information that we hold.
  • Rectification: You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Erasure: You can request that we erase your personal information under certain circumstances.
  • Restriction of processing: You can request that we restrict the processing of your personal information under certain circumstances.
  • Data portability: You can request that we provide your personal information to you in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Object to processing: You can object to the processing of your personal information under certain circumstances.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at skloker@ndejjeuniversity.ac.ug.

Children's Privacy

WASHtsApp is not intended for use by children under the age of 12. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under that age.

Changes to the Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a revised version on this page.
