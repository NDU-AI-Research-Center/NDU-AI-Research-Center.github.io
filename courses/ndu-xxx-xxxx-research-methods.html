Scientific Research and Research Methods (in Information Systems)

About this Course

This course equips students with the knowledge and skills to conduct research, analyze data (both quantitative and qualitative), and present their findings. By the end, students will be able to design research projects and effectively communicate their results.

Course codes: BCS-2204

This is a self-study blended course. You'll find the course materials in the eLearning System. These consist of more than 50 carefully selected resources that come in the form of videos, slides, papers, blog articles, podcasts, and so on. You can work through these materials at your own pace. However, make sure you have completed the relevant lessons before the respective tests. The self-study lessons are accompanied by four in-person, hands-on sessions.

Learning Objectives

On completion of the course students will be expected to:

  • Demonstrate knowledge of how to conduct research that rigourosly answers pre-determined relevant research questions.
  • Experiment with the various data analysis and reporting techniques.
