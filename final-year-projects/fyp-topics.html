About Final-Year-Projects at NDU AI Research Center

As the AI Research Center, we offer opportunities for Final Year Projects. The topic is related to the research domains of the AI Research Center. Therefore, candidates do not come up with a topic by their own, but we develop your topic together, based on your preferences and skills. Therefore, do not spent too much effort in developing something in advance, but first try to talk to us.
